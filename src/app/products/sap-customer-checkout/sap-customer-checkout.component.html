Key Capabilities

Centralized sales processes

  • Manage all point-of-sale functions, such as article selection, discounts, returns, tax deduction, payment methods, and loyalty management.

  • Table management functionality allows you to track table service in catering.

Flexible payment options

  • Pay with cash, credit card, or invoice; integrate a payment terminal; or expand the solution by offering a cashless payment option via plug-ins.

  • With a few clicks, you can split payments, pay in several currencies, and integrate customer orders.

  • Allow loyal consumers to pay using their loyalty points.

Master data management

  • Manage master data for articles, price lists, and customers directly in SAP Customer Checkout manager or in your fully connected ERP system.

  • Through real-time synchronization, data may be updated in less than a minute.

Real-time integration

  • SAP Customer Checkout may be used as a stand-alone solution with a simple installation and configuration process.

  • By connecting the solution with an ERP system, you may send transactions straight to your warehouse management system for same-day analysis.

Accurate, real-time reporting

  • Access real-time reports to see out how much money is made by shop, cashier, or item.

  • Maintain control over your financial performance and obtain all necessary data on the same day.

  • Analyze sales data in SAP Customer Checkout manager, or go further into specific insights for connecting with ERP systems and SAP Analytics Cloud.

Extensibility

  • Extend and customize SAP Client Checkout by utilizing plug-ins to satisfy particular customer needs and fill last-mile gaps.

  • Integrate with additional SAP products, such as SAP Analytics Cloud, to expand your possibilities even further.
